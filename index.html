These authors are already on board!

Josh Robinson

Josh Robinson is a digital artist with 15 years of of industry experience. During that time he and has supervised at companies like Electronic Arts and Lucas film. He is currently working as model supervisor at Double Negative.

Tyrus Goshay

Tyrus recently worked with WMS Games in Chicago and was previously the game Design program Chair at IADT-Chicago. Tyrus as also served as the Senior 3D Lead Supervisor for Foreverinteractive and an Illustrator for Disney books. Tyrus also spent many years designing toys for the McDonalds happy meal program, Cartoon Network, Nickelodeon, Disney and many more.

Henry Darnell

A professional artist working in animated films and visual effects for over 20 years, Henry Darnell has created physical miniatures, puppets and props for film and television. He made the transition to digital (computer) modeling during a twelve year career at Sony Pictures Imageworks.

Doris Fiebig

Doris has a passion for creating portraits ans is fancinated by capturing the moment in time. Doris has an extensive background in pencil and is a sculptor of wood, clay and digitaly with Blender and Zbrush.

Sing Khamnouane

Sing is a talented modeler and sculptor with a Master of Fine Arts and Technology from the University of Texas at Dallas. He’s worked on feature films for Walt Disney Animation including “Tangled”, the 3D Looney Tunes shorts for Warner Bros Animation, and Reel FX’s first feature “Free Birds”. He’s currently working as a modeler at Reel FX on the upcoming Guillermo del Toro produced “The Book of Life” feature animation.

Adam Sacco

Adam Sacco is a freelance 3D Artist and specializes in character design and modeling. He has worked on a variety of animations, video games, promo illustrations and toy prototypes. Adam has had his personal work featured on multiple 3d website and magazines like 3D World magazine for creature design.
